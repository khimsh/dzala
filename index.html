Our Mission

We see Dzala’s future in developing a range of food products which support cellular fitness and wellbeing of the human organism. These functional food products will be based on natural compounds that show a beneficial effect on energetical balance of the cell and in particular, on its major energy producing entity, mitochondria.

We want to approach consumers reaching out for high-quality functional food products that boost health and enhance quality of life. We hope that our products will contribute to ever increasing demand of people to oppose aging and to increase the lifespan.

l Diary product with (Ongoing Project)

We work towards our strategic goal of providing high-content functional food products to consumers containing enriched in both, D-lactate and glycolate. To achieve this objective, we develop methods and processes for glycolate enrichment. In addition, we plan to expand our product pipeline to include non-beverage products.

We develop a fruit-based beverage that contains high amounts of glycolate and  D-lactate derived from natural sources. As we have shown previously, both of these substances are boosting mitochondrial activity significantly.

After collecting various botanicals, we analysed the glycolate content and identified a fruit endemic in Georgia as a suitable starting material. To fortify the preparation with D-lactate, we developed, GMO-free fermentation process, using mix of yoghurt / matsoni producing bacteria (Lactobacilli and Bifidobacteria), including endemic strains from Georgia. Remarkably, we achieved a D-lactate content significantly higher than in dairy products available on the world-wide market. We are finalizing process optimization and expect to make the first product batch available to test customers and partners soon.
